# Research Brief: Advancement Technology Market Sizing

**Sequence:** Run first (market sizing informs segmentation decisions)
**Best Tool:** Perplexity Pro or Gemini Deep Research

---

## Context

I'm building an AI-powered analytics platform for advancement offices. I need to quantify the market opportunity and identify the optimal beachhead segment. The platform works as an intelligence layer on top of existing CRMs.

---

## Primary Research Questions

### 1. Higher Education Market

- How many degree-granting institutions exist in the United States? Break down by:
  - Carnegie Classification (R1, R2, Master's, Baccalaureate, Associate's)
  - Public vs. private
  - Endowment size tiers
- How many of these have formal advancement/development offices?
- What is the typical advancement office budget by institution size?
- What is the typical technology spend within advancement budgets?
- What is the average number of gift officers per institution by tier?

### 2. Healthcare Philanthropy Market

- How many hospital foundations exist in the United States?
- What is the size distribution (by annual fundraising revenue)?
- What technology platforms do hospital foundations typically use?
- How does healthcare philanthropy differ from higher ed advancement in:
  - Donor types (grateful patients vs. alumni)
  - Regulatory considerations (HIPAA)
  - Typical gift sizes and campaign structures

### 3. General Nonprofit Market

- How many 501(c)(3) organizations exist by revenue tier?
- What percentage have formal major gift programs?
- What is the technology adoption curve for smaller nonprofits vs. institutions?

### 4. Technology Spend Data

- What do institutions spend on CRM/advancement technology annually?
- What is the market size for:
  - Fundraising CRM software
  - Prospect research/wealth screening
  - Analytics/BI for advancement
- What are growth rates for these categories?

### 5. CRM Market Share

- What is the market share breakdown for advancement CRMs in higher education?
  - Blackbaud Raiser's Edge / RE NXT
  - Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud
  - Ellucian Advance
  - Slate (admissions crossing into advancement)
  - Other players
- Is market share shifting? What are the trends?

### 6. Segmentation Criteria

Help me identify the ideal initial target segment by analyzing:
- Which segment has the most acute pain (data exists but no analytics)?
- Which segment has budget authority for new tools?
- Which segment has shortest sales cycles?
- Which segment would provide best case studies for expansion?

---

## Output Format

1. **TAM/SAM/SOM model** with assumptions clearly stated
2. **Market segmentation framework** with sizing per segment
3. **Beachhead segment recommendation** with rationale
4. **Data tables** with sources
5. **Key uncertainties and data gaps** flagged
