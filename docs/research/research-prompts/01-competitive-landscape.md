## Context

I'm building an AI-powered analytics platform for university advancement offices (fundraising/development). The platform will serve as an intelligence layer that works with existing CRMs (Blackbaud, Salesforce) rather than replacing them. I need comprehensive competitive intelligence to inform product positioning and feature prioritization.

---

## Primary Research Questions

### 1. Direct Competitors in AI-Powered Advancement/Fundraising

For each of the following companies, provide:
- Core product offering and positioning
- Target market (institution size, vertical focus)
- Pricing model and price points (if available)
- Key features, especially AI/ML capabilities
- Funding history and investor backing
- Notable customers or case studies
- Strengths and vulnerabilities
- Recent product announcements or pivots

**Companies to research:**
- Gravyty (AI fundraising platform)
- EverTrue (advancement CRM/intelligence)
- DonorSearch (prospect research, AI scoring)
- iWave (wealth screening, prospect research)
- Windfall (consumer wealth data)
- Bloomerang (donor retention analytics)
- Ovrture (proposal management)
- ThankView (video donor engagement)
- Fundraise Up (donation optimization)
- Classy (fundraising platform, GoFundMe owned)
- Bonterra (merged entity - EveryAction, Network for Good, etc.)

### 2. Incumbent CRM AI Capabilities

- What AI/ML features has Blackbaud added to Raiser's Edge NXT in 2024-2026?
- What AI capabilities exist in Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud and Einstein for Nonprofits?
- Are incumbents acquiring AI startups or building in-house?

### 3. Adjacent Technology Categories

Map the broader advancement technology ecosystem:
- Wealth screening providers
- Prospect research tools
- Donor engagement platforms
- Alumni engagement platforms
- Campaign management tools
- Matching gift platforms
- Planned giving software

### 4. AI Adoption in Nonprofit Sector (2024-2026 data)

- What percentage of nonprofits/universities are using AI for fundraising?
- What AI use cases are most common (prospect identification, content generation, donor scoring)?
- What are the barriers to AI adoption cited by advancement professionals?
- Are there industry surveys from CASE, AFP, or AHP on technology adoption?

## Output Format

Provide findings in a structured format with:

1. **Executive summary** of competitive dynamics
2. **Detailed profiles** for each direct competitor
3. **Feature comparison matrix** (AI capabilities, integrations, pricing)
4. **Market positioning map** (axes: AI sophistication vs. advancement specialization)
5. **Identified gaps/whitespace opportunities**
6. **Sources with URLs** for verification
